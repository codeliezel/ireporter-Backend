What is a Red-Flag Record?

162460864-ch-UI-update gh-pages A red-flag record is a detailed information provided by you on cases and incidents of corruption
within AFRICA.

By joining WACA, you are assuming a huge responsiblity to strive for ending corruption in Africa,
we are glad you are taking this step to create a red-flag record by bringing any incident or experiences
of corruption to the notice of appropriate authorities and the general public through this medium.

** Never stop to always create a red-flag record in the need of it.
** Never be bullied to not create one.
** Never be tired to create one.

Thank you. 162460864-ch-UI-update
