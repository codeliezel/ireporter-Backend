What is a Red-Flag Record?

A red-flag record is a detailed information provided by you on cases and incidents of corruption
within AFRICA.

By joining WACA, you are assuming a huge responsiblity to strive for ending corruption in Africa,
we are glad you are taking this step to create a red-flag record by bringing any incident or experiences
of corruption to the notice of appropriate authorities and the general public through this medium.

** Never stop to always create a red-flag record in the need of it.
** Never be bullied to not create one.
** Never be tired to create one.

Thank you.

Why you should always create a Red-Flag Record?

WACA is all about bringing multiple voices together, this medium aims
at creating an opportunity for people's voices to be heard,
people who have been belittled, suffered from and surpressed with corruption.
By creating a red-flag record, an experience will always be a
plus to the movement.
So never hesistate.
