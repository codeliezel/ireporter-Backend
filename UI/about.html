ABOUT WACA

Corruption is a huge bane to Africa's development. African countries must develop novel and localised solutions that will curb this menace, hence the birth of WACA iReporter. WACA iReporter enables any/every citizen to bring any form of corruption to the notice of appropriate authorities and the general public and can also report on things that needs government intervention.

HIGHLIGHTS

What is a Red-flag Record?


A red-flag record is a detailed information provided by you on cases
and incidents of corruption within AFRICA.

By joining WACA, you are assuming a huge responsiblity to strive for ending corruption in Africa,
we are glad you are taking this step to create a red-flag record by bringing any incident or experiences
of corruption to the notice of appropriate authorities and the general public through this medium.

** Never stop to always create a red-flag record in the need of it.
** Never be bullied to not create one.
** Never be tired to create one.

What is Intervention Record?


An intervention record is the call for government agencies
to intervene in cases of corruption, lack of infrastructural facilities,
high rise of poverty, poor healthcare system and many other
problems within AFRICA.

By joining WACA, you are assuming a huge responsiblity to strive for ending corruption in Africa,
we are glad you are taking this step to create an intervention record by sharing your ideas,
reporting on things that needs government intervention and striving to bring cases of
corruption to the notice of government agencies.

Never give up on the fight, your voice is needed!
